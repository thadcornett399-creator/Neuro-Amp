- **Mitigation**: Optimize Web Audio worklets, offer desktop app alternative

**Risk**: Market too niche for sustainable business
- **Mitigation**: Expand to vocals, podcasting, other instruments

- **Mitigation**: Test pricing, add must-have premium features, strong freemium balance

- **Mitigation**: Focus on B2C first, education as bonus revenue stream

- **Mitigation**: Accept outside contributions, consider co-founder, pace development

**Risk**: Feature creep delays launch
- **Mitigation**: Ship MVP, iterate based on feedback, use roadmap to prioritize
